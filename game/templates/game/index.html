Welcome to Cat Words. How to play...

Category words is a fun word game for all ages.

First, you will pick a category, then you will need to keep trying to guess words related to that category until the timer runs out!

The goal is to guess a word that starts with the last letter of the previous word guessed. Guess more words and your score will go up.

Good luck!
